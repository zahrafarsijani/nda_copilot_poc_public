Preferred:
- Timing/Efforts: use “promptly” and “reasonable care”.
- Notices: email sufficient; avoid courier-only.
- CI scope: 6-month lookback + forward disclosures.
- AI tools: allow enterprise no-train; forbid model training.
- Disposal: return or destroy promptly; allow legal holds.
- Indemnities: none; prevailing-party fees only.
- Term: 3 years preferred; 2 acceptable; trade secrets while secret.
Reject:
- “immediately” / “best efforts”
- standstill or exclusivity clauses
- categorical AI prohibitions
- indemnities for indirect/special damages
